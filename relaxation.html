Relaxation Tips for Students

Feeling overwhelmed with classes, exams, or assignments? Here are simple ways to calm down:

  • 🎧 Listen to soft music or nature sounds
  • ✍ Write your worries in a journal
  • 🚶 Take a short walk outside between study sessions
  • 😴 Get enough sleep — it boosts focus & reduces anxiety
  • 📞 Talk to a friend instead of bottling emotions
